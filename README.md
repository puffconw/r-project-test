## 📌 Project Overview  
This project analyzes the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) Toxics Release Inventory (TRI) 2024 dataset. The goal is to explore patterns of toxic chemical releases across U.S. states and examine differences by release pathways (e.g., air vs. water).

---

## 🎯 Research Question  
How do toxic chemical releases vary across U.S. states and release pathways (air vs. water), and what patterns can be observed in environmental exposure?
